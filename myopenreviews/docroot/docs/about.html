A membic is a bite sized structured summary of a noteworthy experience. Noting a membic enhances your memory and feeds into the global discovery stream. Tune your discovery stream to match who you trust and what you like.

Features:

  • Free and open source
  • Automatically track your recent and top posts
  • Your own generated blog page
  • Curated theme groups
  • Remember posts
  • Search posts
  • Prioritize who you hear from
  • Filter by type
  • Easily post books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities or anything else with a URL or physical location
  • Permalinks for easy sharing

If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, open an issue on GitHub.
