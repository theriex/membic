Your theme hashtag link is the easiest and most direct path for accessing theme membics. Define a unique hashtag in your theme settings to enable the link. Remember to choose something that will be unique within the social media you use.

Your theme permalink is a direct url that will never change, so it can be safely shared and published. It can also be embedded inside a website, or even set up as a site by itself.

Embedding

If you already have a website, and would like to set up the theme permalink page as a page inside of your site, just copy the embedding code from the theme settings. For an example of what that can look like, check out the "tech news" section on http://sandservices.com

Standalone Site

To make a theme into a standalone site, check the instructions from your domain registrar on how to set up a masked redirect URL. Enter the permalink for your theme as the URL to use. When people go to the domain you registered, their browser will show the content from your theme. For an example of what that can look like, check out http://epnewtech.com
