The permalink page for your theme has a direct url that will not change, so it can be easily shared and published. It can also be embedded inside your own website, or even set it up as a site by itself.

Embedding

If you already have a website, and would like to set up the theme permalink page as a page inside of your site, just copy the embedding code from the theme settings. For an example of what that can look like, check out http://sandservices.com/test/epnewtech.html

Standalone Site

To make a theme into a standalone site, check the instructions from your domain registrar on how to set up a masked redirect URL. Enter the permalink for your theme as the URL to use. When people go to the domain you registered, their browser will show the content from your theme. For an example of what that can look like, check out http://epnewtech.com
