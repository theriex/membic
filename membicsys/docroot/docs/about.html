membic = Link + Why Memorable.
 Membic.com is a tool for collaborative link sharing. Membic themes are microsites with their own membership.

  • The origin story
  • How it works

Features:

  • Keep your search engine
    On membic.com you choose people and themes you trust, then browse their curated links and why they are memorable.
  • Keep your social net
    You don't need another social net, you need a separate collaboration space. Why?
  • Pen names
    Your pen name can reveal as much or as little of yourself as you want. Creative pen names are welcome. Your email address is never shared with anyone else.
  • Cooperative themes
    Founders, moderators and members can post membics to themes dedicated to specific interests. Themes support RSS and can be embedded into other websites.
  • Curated community
    Not only are you seeing only the links other people found memorable and why, you can prefer, background, or block people to optimize your community view. Follow specific themes you are interested in for easy access from your profile.
  • Open keyword tagging
    Tag membics using the standard keyword checkboxes, write in your own, or both. Add custom theme-specific keywords.
  • Tracking and memory
    Automatically track your recent favorite membics for easy access. Remember membics from others, or mark your own membics as future to follow up on later.

FAQ:

  • Are you going to spam me?
    No. Your email address is confirmed with the server to help verify you are a human (or at least a modestly well written robot). We send you mail if you request your password, or if someone who already knows your email address asks us to send you an invitation to collaborate on a theme they are writing. We don't share your email with anyone ever.
  • What should I start with?
    Try making membics for the last five things you encountered that were worth remembering. Books are always impressive.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Bookmarklet

    Firefox: "Right click" the 'Make Membic' link below and add it to your bookmarks.
    Chrome/Safari: "Right click" the 'Make Membic' link below, copy the link address, make a new bookmark, then paste the URL.

    Make Membic

    The next time you are on a memorable page, select "Make Membic" from your bookmarks and the script will launch membic in a new browser window.

  • Mail In Membics

    You can make a membic by sending mail to: membic@membicsys.appspotmail.com

    Describe why memorable in the subject line. In the body, the first line starting with "http://" or "https://" is used for the URL. Mailing in will create a future membic you can fill out next time you are online. Find mailed-in membics on the remembered tab of your profile.

  • iTunes® playlists

    If you would like to build playlists from keywords and update your top music membics automatically from iTunes® check out the situational playlist generator project on github.
