A membic is a bite sized structured summary of a noteworthy experience. When you discover something worth remembering, make a membic.

Features:

  • 7+other primary categorization
  • Open keyword tagging
  • Easily post solid synopses of books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else with a URL or physical location.
  • Track your recent and top membics.
  • Search by name, filter by type.
  • Follow and join cooperative themes.
  • Embed themes into other web pages.
  • Prioritize sources.
  • Remember interesting membics.
  • Creative pen names welcome.
  • Free and open source.

If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of this site, open an issue on GitHub.
