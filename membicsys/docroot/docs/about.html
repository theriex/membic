A membic is a bite sized structured summary of a noteworthy experience. When you find something worth remembering, make a membic.

membic.com is a collaborative membic project. ➡ Read how it works

Features:

  • 7+other primary categorization
    Easily post solid synopses of books, articles, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else with a URL or physical location. Easily filter and browse common membic types. For more specific interests, join or create a cooperative theme.
  • Open keyword tagging
    Tag membics using the type optimized common keyword checkboxes, write in your own, or both.
  • Tracking and memory
    Automatically track your recent favorite membics for easy access. Remember membics from others to look into later.
  • Optimized feed
    Prefer, background, or block people to optimize your view of the community feed. Follow specific themes you are interested in.
  • Cooperative themes
    Founders, moderators and members can post membics through to cooperative themes set up for specific interests. Follow a theme to access it directly from your profile. Themes support RSS and can be embedded into other websites.
  • Pen names
    Impressions are public, but your pen name can reveal as much or as little of yourself as you want. Creative pen names are welcome, and you can have more than one. Your email address is never shared with anyone else. Membic.com is open source.

FAQ:

  • Are you going to spam me?
    No. We send you email to verify your account and then if you forgot your password. We don't share your email with anyone ever.
  • What should I start with?
    Try making membics for your favorite books. Books are a substantive part of your profile. If you have a particular hobby or special interest, create a theme for it.
