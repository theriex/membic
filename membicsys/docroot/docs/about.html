A membic is a bite sized structured summary of a noteworthy experience. Noting a membic enhances your memory and feeds the discovery stream.

On our own, the quality of our total experiences is limited. By considering what others have noted, quality can be improved. Through groups devoted to particular themes, quality can be maximized.

Features:

  • Track your recent and top membics.
  • Prioritize who you hear from.
  • Follow or join curated groups.
  • Embed groups in other web pages.
  • Remember interesting membics.
  • 7+other primary categorization
  • Open keyword tagging
  • Search by name, filter by type.
  • Easily post solid synopses of books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else with a URL or physical location.
  • Free and open source.

If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, open an issue on GitHub.
