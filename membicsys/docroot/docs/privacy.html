Our commitment to privacy:

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The information we collect:

membic.com collects general descriptive information including item identification field values, star values, keywords and other descriptive text you provide. These data are saved with a reference to a unique pen name created by you. Pen names, membics and themes are publicly visible.

membic.com supports native email/password login. Information collected in support of the login process is not publicly available. Your email address is never shared by the site.

How we use information:

We use the information we collect to help you organize your membics and make them generally accessible in a variety of formats.

Our commitment to data security:

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our commitment to children's privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.
