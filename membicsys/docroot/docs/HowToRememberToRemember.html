How To

Augmented Memory

Augmenting your memory with membic is fantastic, but to get the benefits, you need to make membics. That means integrating membic with how you roll. The two main ways to do that on your phone or computer are copy-paste or mail-in.

Copy-Paste Membics

  • On your phone or tablet:

    1. Click the share button on your browser and select "copy link".
    2. Open membic, click , paste the url and click "Read".

    It can be helpful to have membic on your home screen, that way you can just click home and open membic.

  • On your computer:

    1. Select the url in your browser and copy it.
    2. Open membic, click , paste the url and click "Read".

    It can be helpful to add membic to your bookmarks/favorites for quick access. Or try out the bookmarklet (see below).

Mail-In Membics

  • On your phone or tablet:

    1. Click the share button on your browser and select mail.
    2. Send to me@membic.org

    Membic will email you back with a link to confirm your post.

  • On your computer:

    1. Share from your browser, or select the url and copy it.
    2. Send the link in an email to me@membic.org

    Membic will email you back with a link to confirm your post.

Remembering Books

To remember a book, just click and start typing the title. When you see the right book show up in the auto-complete list, select it to automatically fill out all the fields.

Extras

  • The Membic Bookmarklet is a tiny snippet of code that takes the URL of any page you are viewing and passes it along to Membic in a new browser tab. After installing, you can select "Make Membic" from your bookmarks to start writing a membic for any page you are on. Here's how to install the bookmarklet:
    • Firefox
      1. "Right click" this link Make Membic and choose "Bookmark This Link".
    • Chrome/Safari
      1. "Right click" this link Make Membic and choose "Copy Link Address".
      2. Bookmarks | Bookmark This Page...
      3. Edit... change the bookmark name to "Make Membic", replace the URL with the copied link. Save.
    The next time you are reading something memorable, select "Make Membic" from your bookmarks to make a membic for it.
