Posting Membics to Social Media

When you write a membic, it might also be of general interest to people in your social network. Membic.com uses social network publishing technology from AddToAny to keep up with the rapidly evolving landscape of capabilities and publishing requirements. Here's one approach that works well:

  1. If you don't have one already, create a Twitter account where you will share membics.
  2. If you are posting membics from a theme, set a hashtag in your theme settings. Choose something relatively unique, like your web domain.
  3. Connect your Facebook account to your Twitter account.
  4. After you click the save button for a new membic, click the Tweet button in the displayed share options.

For many more social network sharing options, click the + button.

If you don't see any share buttons at all, you may be running an older browser, or your privacy settings are turned up high enough so that the AddToAny code was blocked. Social sharing may require browsing history to be enabled.

Posting a Theme to Social Media

To share a theme, click the stacked menu next to the theme name to display the share buttons.
