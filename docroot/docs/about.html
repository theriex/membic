Friends Guiding Friends

What aspects were good? What aspects were not? Was it worth your time? Would you recommend it to a friend?

When friends describe an experience, it's worth reading. Friends help you find new things you might enjoy.

Who this is for

FGFweb is a hype-phobic source of trusted information shared at comfortable levels of anonymity. FGFweb values depth of expression and does not overwhelm you. With FGFweb you can:

  • Automatically track your current top 20 favorite experiences.
  • Share your personal log page.
  • Create and join groups devoted to specific themes.
  • See recent and top posts from friends.
  • Remember or pre-review things you want to check out later.
  • Track and search your posts. Track and search posts from your friends.
  • Filter what types of posts you read from whom.
  • Get introduced to new friends through advanced search.
  • Easily post about books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else with a URL or physical location.
  • Share your posts anywhere you can post a link.

FGFweb is part of MyOpenReviews, a social review platform that respects your privacy. Both real names and creative names are welcome, reputation is built on tips and insights.

FGFweb will always be free. Your involvement is appreciated. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, please open an issue on the GitHub project.
