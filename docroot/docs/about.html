Welcome to a community of pen names where you can share reviews of things to read, watch, listen to, eat, drink and more. Follow people whose tastes you trust, inspire others to experience new things, and build relationships through shared experiences.

wdydfun is part of MyOpenReviews, an open source platform for social review sharing. The goal is to enable sharing detailed impressions without requiring a full dossier of personal details. Here creative names are as valid as real names, and your reputation is built on the reviews you share. Check in at your own pace.

wdydfun will always be free. The site is supported by limited advertising, sales commissions and voluntary contributions. It is open to participation at all levels.

Contact: If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, or if you have a question, or if you bump into something that doesn't work right, please open an issue on the GitHub project
