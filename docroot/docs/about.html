MyOpenReviews helps build shared experiences between you and friends whose taste you trust. Share reviews of books, movies, videos, music, food, drink, activities and more. Easily create a review and post through to your social networks, while automatically tracking your top 20s and taking advantage of other collaborative features.

This is a community of pen names, so you can reveal as much or as little about yourself as you want. Check in at your own pace.

  • More features and application add-ons

MyOpenReviews is a community of reviewers supported by a community of developers, and it is open to participation at all levels. If you see something strange or have a suggestion, please open an issue on the GitHub project.
