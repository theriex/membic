When friends write reviews, it's worth reading to hear their reactions. Friends help you experience new things you might enjoy.

WDYDFun is a high quality social review site featuring personal review logs, themed groups, automatic top 20 lists, memory links and more. Share on Facebook, Twitter, and anywhere you can post a link.

WDYDFun is part of MyOpenReviews, an open source social review logging platform that enables trusted sharing of detailed impressions while respecting your privacy. Both real names and creative names are ok here, reputation is built on tips and insights. Check in at your own pace.

WDYDFun will always be free.

WDYDFun is open to participation at all levels. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, or if you have a question, or if you bump into something that doesn't work right, open an issue on the GitHub project
