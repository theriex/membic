MyOpenReviews helps people build shared experiences around fun things to do, food, drink, music, videos, movies, books and anything else that can be reviewed. When you review something, you can post the review through to your social networks, and anyone following you can see what you've checked out recently. Likewise you can see what your friends have been into.

What makes MyOpenReviews different is that in addition to activity feeds, you can also check out your friend's updated top 20 best reviews of all time, and click to remember any review you want to check out later. MyOpenReviews is a community of pen names, so you can reveal as much or as little about yourself as you want.

You can use the MyOpenReviews bookmarklet to quickly create a review from any page on the internet.

MyOpenReviews is currently pre-Alpha. Many basic features are available, but it is not fully tested and there are still things left to do. This is a community of reviewers supported by a community of developers. It is a work in progress, and open to participation. If you run into anything strange or have a suggestion, please open an issue on the GitHub project.
