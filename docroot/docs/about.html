MyOpenReviews is a "follow" style social net built over a powerful review creation and management platform.

You use MyOpenReviews to bridge between experiencing things and communicating those experiences. For example when you read a book, you can write a quick review of it, then share that review with your friends. Your reviews are saved for easy access, so you and your friends can always find what books you've been into. MyOpenReviews supports books, movies, videos, music, food, drink and other stuff.

You write reviews under a chosen pen name, so you can reveal as much or as little of yourself as you want.

This is a community of reviewers supported by a community of developers. It is a work in progress, and open to participation. New connection services are being developed and existing capabilities improved. Check it out.

MyOpenReviews is currently pre-Alpha. Many basic features are available, but it is not fully tested and there are still things left to do. If you run into anything strange or have a suggestion, please leave a note on the GitHub project. It would be great to hear from you.
