What aspects were good? What aspects were not? Was it worth your time? Would you repeat the experience? Would you recommend it to others?

When friends tell us these things, it's worth reading. Sometimes it's worth checking out new stuff, other times it's just fun hearing our friend's reactions. What did you do fun?

WDYDFun helps your reviews do more good through your own personal review log, themed groups, automatic top 20 lists and more. Share on Facebook, Twitter, and anywhere you can post a permalink for your review.

WDYDFun is part of MyOpenReviews, an open source social review logging platform that enables trusted sharing of detailed impressions without invading your privacy. Both real names and creative names are valid. Reputation is built on tips and insights. Check in at your own pace.

WDYDFun will always be free.

WDYDFun is open to participation at all levels. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, or if you have a question, or if you bump into something that doesn't work right, open an issue on the GitHub project
