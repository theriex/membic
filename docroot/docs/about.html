MyOpenReviews helps build shared experiences between you and friends whose tastes you trust. You can review fun activities, food, drink, music, videos, movies, books and more. MyOpenReviews automatically updates your top 20 lists, shows recent reviews from your friends, and keeps track of reviews you've remembered.

This is a community of pen names, so you can reveal as much or as little about yourself as you want. Check in at your own pace.

To use MyOpenReviews, just write a review and post through to your existing social networks.

More features and application add-ons

MyOpenReviews is a community of reviewers supported by a community of developers, and it is open to participation at all levels. If you see something strange or have a suggestion, please open an issue on the GitHub project.
