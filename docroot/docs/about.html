Welcome to a community of pen names where you can share reviews of things to read, watch, hear, eat, drink and more. Follow people whose tastes you trust, inspire others to experience new things, and build relationships through shared experiences.

wdydfun is part of MyOpenReviews, an open source platform for social review sharing. The goal of the platform is to enable trusted sharing of detailed impressions without requiring a dossier of personal details. Creative names are as valid as real names, and reputation is built on tips and insights. Check in at your own pace.

wdydfun will always be free.

wdydfun is open to participation at all levels. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, or if you have a question, or if you bump into something that doesn't work right, please open an issue on the GitHub project
