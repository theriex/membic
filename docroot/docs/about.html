WDYDFun is social reviews.

What aspects were good? What aspects were not? Was it worth your time? Would you repeat the experience? Would you recommend it?

When friends write reviews, it's worth reading to hear their reactions. Friends help you experience new things you might enjoy.

WDYDFun is a social review site featuring personal review logs, themed groups, automatic top 20 lists, memory links and more. Share on Facebook, Twitter, and anywhere you can post a link.

WDYDFun is part of MyOpenReviews, a social review platform that respects your privacy. Both real names and creative names are welcome, reputation is built on tips and insights. Check in at your own pace.

WDYDFun will always be free.

WDYDFun welcomes your feedback. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, open an issue on the GitHub project
