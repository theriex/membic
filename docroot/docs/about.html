WDYDFun is social reviews.

What aspects were good? What aspects were not? Was it worth your time? Would you repeat the experience? Would you recommend it?

When friends write reviews, it's worth reading to hear their reactions. Friends help you experience new things you might enjoy.

Who this is for

WDYDFun is a hype-phobic source of trusted information shared at comfortable levels of anonymity. If you can articulate your experiences, slow-steady networking values your thoughts as much as what you choose to review. With WDYDFun you can:

  • Automatically track your current top 20 favorite experiences.
  • Share your personal review log page with your recent and top posts.
  • Create and join groups devoted to specific themes.
  • See recent and top reviews from friends.
  • Remember or pre-review things you want to check out later.
  • Track and search your reviews. Track and search reviews from your friends.
  • Fine tune which types of reviews you follow from each of your friends.
  • Get introduced to new friends through advanced search.
  • Easily review books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else that has a URL or physical location.
  • Share your reviews anywhere you can post a URL.

WDYDFun is part of MyOpenReviews, a social review platform that respects your privacy. Both real names and creative names are welcome, reputation is built on tips and insights.

WDYDFun will always be free. Your involvement is appreciated. If you have a suggestion to increase the awesomeness of the site, please open an issue on the GitHub project.
