MyOpenReviews is a place to log your experiences and share them with your friends.

  • Review books, movies, videos, music, food, drinks, activities and anything else in one place.
  • Write reviews for your friends and followers, read reviews from people that matter to you.
  • Not limited to items for sale, sensitive account information not required.

This is a community of pen names supported by a community of developers. It is open to participation at all levels. If you find a a bug or have a suggestion or question, please open an issue on the GitHub project. Check in at your own pace.
