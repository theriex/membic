MyOpenReviews is an open hub for reviews. The goal is to be able to keep all your reviews in one place and then easily distribute them. It's basically an experiment to address some nagging questions like

  1. If I listen to a song on one site and note that I like it, can that propagate through to the other music services I use? Can I track all of that in one place?
  2. Is it possible to manage my reviews so my friends or myself can easily find my favorite songs of all time, and what I've been into recently? Can that also work for books and other things?
  3. Can networking about media be a better way to find things I would probably like? Is it possible to network without tying my real life professional identity to everything?
  4. Will better tools make it worth reviewing more things? Is it cool to have a library of impressions? Can a smart program automatically find things for me based on my mood or what I'm doing?

Stuff like that. Want to see how it goes? Join up!
