Our commitment to privacy:

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The information we collect:

MyOpenReviews collects general rating information including item identification, slider values, comments, tags and attributes you provide. These ratings are saved under a unique pen name created by you. The pen name and all review details are publicly visible.

MyOpenReviews supports login via username and password, or through third party authentication services. All information collected in support of the login process is private and visible only to you. Things like your email address, social networking account ids etc are private and not visible to others.

How we use information:

We use the information we collect to help you track your media experiences, share them with friends, retrieve review information from other sites, and publish reviews to other sites.

Retrieving reviews from other sites, or publishing reviews to other sites, may require additional login processing. This login information is private and shared only with the other site as needed to transfer review information.

Our commitment to data security:

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our commitment to children's privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.
